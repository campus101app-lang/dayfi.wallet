Good to know,
before you start.

DayFi is a non-custodial crypto wallet built for everyday people. It lets you send and receive USDC and XLM on the Stellar network using a simple @dayfi.me username — no complicated wallet addresses required.

Your recovery phrase is 12 words that give complete access to your wallet. Write them down and keep them safe offline. Anyone with these words can access your funds — and without them, your funds cannot be recovered.

Go to Security in the app menu and tap "Back up wallet." You'll be shown your 12-word recovery phrase. Write it down and store it somewhere safe. You can also save an encrypted copy to cloud storage.

Tap "Receive" to get your @dayfi.me username or QR code. Share it with anyone. To send, tap "Send," enter the recipient's @dayfi.me username or Stellar address, choose an amount, and confirm. Transactions settle in under 5 seconds.

Stellar network fees are fractions of a cent — typically 0.00001 XLM per transaction. DayFi itself charges no additional fees. You'll need a small XLM balance (minimum 2 XLM) to keep your wallet active.

DayFi currently supports USDC (USD Coin by Circle) and XLM (Stellar Lumens) on the Stellar mainnet. More assets and networks are coming soon.

Visit Settings in the app to manage your account. For support, reach us at dayfi.co/support. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
